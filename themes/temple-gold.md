# Temple Gold

A sacred and opulent theme inspired by South Indian temple architecture, featuring antique gold and deep bronze.

## Color Palette

- **Antique Gold**: `#b89428` - Gilded temple domes
- **Deep Bronze**: `#6b4428` - Sacred bells and artifacts
- **Sacred Red**: `#a82f2f` - Kumkum and vermilion
- **Temple Cream**: `#f5ebd8` - Stone sanctum walls

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Serif

## Best Used For

Religious content, cultural heritage presentations, jewelry brands, traditional crafts, spiritual content, luxury ethnic brands.
