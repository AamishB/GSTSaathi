# Spice Market

A vibrant and aromatic theme inspired by Indian spice bazaars, featuring turmeric, cardamom, and chili tones.

## Color Palette

- **Turmeric Yellow**: `#f4a300` - Golden spice powder
- **Cardamom Green**: `#8f9e5a` - Aromatic seed pods
- **Kashmiri Chili Red**: `#c41e3a` - Bold spice heat
- **Cumin Brown**: `#8b5a2b` - Earthy spice base

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Food and culinary brands, restaurant menus, cooking shows, spice companies, ethnic food products, cultural festivals.
