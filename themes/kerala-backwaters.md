# Kerala Backwaters

A tropical and tranquil theme inspired by South India's serene backwaters, coconut groves, and lagoons.

## Color Palette

- **Coconut Palm Green**: `#3d6b4f` - Lush tropical foliage
- **Lagoon Blue**: `#5fa3a3` - Calm backwater waters
- **Golden Coconut**: `#c9a959` - Sun-kissed accents
- **Ivory White**: `#fffff0` - Clean tropical light

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Travel and tourism, wellness retreats, eco-friendly brands, tropical destinations, Ayurveda and spa content, environmental presentations.
