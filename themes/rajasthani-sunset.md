# Rajasthani Sunset

A regal and warm theme inspired by the desert palaces of Rajasthan, featuring royal pinks and golden sandstone architecture.

## Color Palette

- **Royal Pink**: `#c75b7a` - Jaipur's iconic color
- **Saffron Orange**: `#e87b3a` - Desert sunset hues
- **Sandstone Gold**: `#d4a574` - Historic palace walls
- **Cream White**: `#f8f4e8` - Marble accents

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Heritage brands, luxury hospitality, cultural presentations, travel and tourism, artisan crafts, royal-themed events.
