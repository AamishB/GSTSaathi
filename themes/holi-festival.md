# Holi Festival

A vibrant and joyful theme inspired by the Festival of Colors, featuring electric pinks, blues, and marigold yellows.

## Color Palette

- **Electric Pink**: `#ff6bb3` - Gulal powder bursts
- **Sky Blue**: `#4da8da` - Celebratory color clouds
- **Marigold Yellow**: `#ffc93c` - Festive flower garlands
- **Pure White**: `#ffffff` - Clean canvas for colors

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Festival promotions, youth-oriented content, creative campaigns, colorful product launches, celebration events, social media content.
