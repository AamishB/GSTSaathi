# Kashmir Valley

A serene and majestic theme inspired by the Himalayan landscapes, featuring snow whites, chinar reds, and lake blues.

## Color Palette

- **Snow White**: `#f8f8ff` - Pristine Himalayan peaks
- **Chinar Red**: `#c44536` - Autumn maple leaves
- **Dal Lake Blue**: `#5b7c99` - Serene valley waters
- **Pine Green**: `#2d4a3e` - Evergreen mountain forests

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Travel and tourism, mountain destinations, environmental content, peaceful retreats, nature photography, outdoor adventure brands.
