# Diwali Lights

A warm and celebratory theme inspired by the Festival of Lights, featuring glowing diyas and festive prosperity.

## Color Palette

- **Deep Maroon**: `#800020` - Rich ceremonial base
- **Antique Gold**: `#c9a959` - Glowing diya flames
- **Warm Amber**: `#e8a84a` - Candlelit radiance
- **Ivory Cream**: `#fffdd0` - Soft festive backgrounds

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Festival promotions, celebration announcements, luxury brands, wedding content, cultural events, financial prosperity themes.
