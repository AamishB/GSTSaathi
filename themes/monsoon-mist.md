# Monsoon Mist

A serene and refreshing theme inspired by the Indian monsoon season, capturing the essence of rain-washed landscapes and petrichor.

## Color Palette

- **Cloud Grey**: `#6b7b8c` - Cool monsoon cloud tones
- **Rain Washed Green**: `#87a878` - Fresh foliage after rain
- **Misty Blue**: `#a8b8c8` - Soft overcast skies
- **Pearl White**: `#f5f5f0` - Clean, fresh backgrounds

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Sans Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Weather-related content, environmental presentations, wellness and spa brands, calming corporate decks, sustainability reports.
