# Mughal Gardens

An elegant and sophisticated theme inspired by Persian-style Mughal architecture, featuring jade greens and marble elegance.

## Color Palette

- **Jade Green**: `#00a86b` - Lush garden cypress trees
- **Lapis Lazuli**: `#26619c` - Intricate tile work
- **Marble White**: `#f0e8dc` - Pristine palace surfaces
- **Sandstone Beige**: `#d4c4a8` - Historic foundations

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Serif

## Best Used For

Historical presentations, architecture portfolios, luxury brands, cultural heritage content, museum exhibitions, academic lectures.
