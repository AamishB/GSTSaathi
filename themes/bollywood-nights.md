# Bollywood Nights

A glamorous and dramatic theme inspired by Indian cinema's glitz, featuring rich purples and sequin gold.

## Color Palette

- **Royal Purple**: `#5a2d82` - Dramatic velvet curtains
- **Sequin Gold**: `#d4af37` - Award show sparkle
- **Crimson Red**: `#9f1d35` - Red carpet elegance
- **Midnight Black**: `#1a1a2e` - Theater ambiance

## Typography

- **Headers**: DejaVu Serif Bold
- **Body Text**: DejaVu Sans

## Best Used For

Entertainment industry, award ceremonies, film promotions, fashion shows, nightlife events, celebrity content, luxury brands.
